Credit Climb

Roll through early adulthood, build your credit, unlock better opportunities, and reach Financial Freedom before your rivals do.

Rulebook and Foundations of Life

How A Turn Works

1. You collect income and cover recurring bills.

2. Your credit shifts based on payments, utilization, debt pressure, and negative marks.

3. You roll the dice and land on a life, credit, housing, transport, crisis, or growth space.

4. Your choices shape savings, debt, and milestone progress.

Foundations of Life

Stability: housing and an emergency fund.

Mobility: transportation that is useful and not a trap.

Growth: a side hustle, investing, or another future asset.

Security: strong credit and surviving setbacks without collapse.

How You Win

Reach Financial Freedom by finishing all six goals shown in the roadmap panel: stability, mobility, growth, security, savings, and debt control.
